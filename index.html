How it works

  1. Submit your inspiration: Upload your Pinterest fashion images and provide details about your desired outfit.
  2. Get matched with tailors: We connect you with skilled tailors who can bring your vision to life.
  3. Collaborate on design: Work closely with your tailor to finalize the design, choose fabrics, and ensure a perfect fit.
  4. Receive your custom outfit: Your tailor will create and deliver your custom-made outfit, tailored to your specifications.
